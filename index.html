Carlos's favorite restaurants

By: Carlos Rodriguez

This is a list of my favorite restaurants

Name: El Celler

location: Alto Prado Cra. 53 #79-127, Barranquilla, Atlántico

Menu description: El Celler specializes in Mediterranean cuisine, offering a mix of Spanish and international dishes. Their menu features a variety of seafood, tapas, and paella, along with an extensive wine list to complement the flavors.

Name: Varadero

location: El Prado Cra. 51 #79-97, Barranquilla, Atlántico

Menu description: Varadero is a Cuban-inspired seafood restaurant, known for its fresh fish and shellfish dishes. Popular options include ceviche, grilled octopus, and lobster, all with a Caribbean twist, served in a vibrant and lively atmosphere.
